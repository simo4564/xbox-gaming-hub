Privacy Policy

Gaming Community collects only the information required to process and support your digital order, such as your selected items, region, gamer tag, contact email, and WhatsApp request details.

We do not sell your personal data. Information shared during checkout is used only to confirm your order, deliver support, and resolve activation issues if needed.

When you use the website, some settings such as cart contents, favorites, and recently viewed items may be stored locally in your browser for a smoother shopping experience.

If you want any order-related data removed from active support conversations, contact us directly.
