Game Pass & Subscription Plans

Microsoft restructured Xbox Game Pass in October 2025, creating four tiers and increasing prices—Ultimate now costs $29.99 per month. The plans below summarise the current offerings; pick the tier that best suits your gaming habits.

Plan Price (monthly) Key Features
Essential $9.99 50+ curated games; online multiplayer; basic cloud gaming access.
Premium $14.99 200+ games across console and PC; first‑party titles arrive approximately 12 months later; Call of Duty excluded.
PC Game Pass $16.49 PC‑only; includes day‑one releases (including Call of Duty); EA Play and Ubisoft+ Classics; no cloud gaming on mobile or TV.
Ultimate $29.99 Bundles EA Play, Ubisoft+ Classics and Fortnite Crew; day‑one releases; cloud streaming up to 1440p; 75+ new titles per year.

Only the Ultimate and PC Game Pass tiers include day‑one first‑party releases such as Call of Duty.
