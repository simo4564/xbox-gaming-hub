Game Pass Plans

Microsoft introduced new tiers in October 2025, upgrading Core subscribers to Essential and Standard subscribers to Premium. Ultimate now costs $29.99/month while Premium remains $14.99 and Essential $9.99.

Plan Monthly Price Key Features
Essential $9.99 A curated library of around 50 games, online console multiplayer and basic cloud gaming.
Premium $14.99 Hundreds of console and PC games; first‑party titles arrive about a year after launch; Call of Duty excluded.
PC Game Pass $16.49 PC‑only library with day‑one releases (including Call of Duty), EA Play and Ubisoft+ Classics; no mobile/TV cloud gaming.
Ultimate $29.99 Everything in Premium plus day‑one releases; includes EA Play, Ubisoft+ Classics and Fortnite Crew; enhanced cloud streaming up to 1440p and over 75 new titles annually.

Day‑one games are only included with Game Pass Ultimate and PC Game Pass. Premium subscribers play first‑party games around a year after launch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Game Pass Ultimate worth the price?

Ultimate offers day‑one releases, EA Play, Ubisoft+ Classics and cloud streaming up to 1440p. If you want access to everything, it’s the best value.

How do I redeem digital codes purchased here?

After checkout, you’ll receive codes by email. Redeem them on your Xbox console or at redeem.microsoft.com.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my Game Pass plan later?

Yes—Microsoft allows you to change your subscription tier at any time through your account settings.
